Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from other Google Maps scrapers?
Most scrapers only grab data visible on the map card (Name, Address, generic phone). We go a step further by visiting the business's actual website to crawl for email addresses, social media links, and other metadata, giving you a complete lead profile.
Do I need to know how to code?
Not at all. The interface is visual and intuitive. Just fill in the search fields, check the boxes for the data you want, and click Start. We handle the technical heavy lifting.
How much does it cost to run?
We use the Apify platform's transparent pricing. You only pay for the compute units you use. In general, you can scrape thousands of leads for just a few dollars—significantly cheaper than buying pre-made lists.
Can I use this for AI agents?
Yes! This is one of the few scrapers with native Model Context Protocol (MCP) support. This means you can plug it directly into LLM workflows (like Claude or OpenAI) to automate data gathering tasks.
